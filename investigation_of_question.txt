problem advent of code 2025 day 4 printing department

given a 2d grid
@ means roll of paper
. means empty

each cell has 8 neighobours including diagonals

a roll of paper is accessible if less than 4 of its neighboring cells are @

part 1
from the original grid count how many paper rolls are accessible

part 2
start again from the original grid and repeat
find all accessible paper rolls
remove all of them together by turning @ into .
recalculate accessibility on the new grid

stop when no paper roll is accessible

output
part 1 number of accessible rolls in the initial grid
part 2 total number of rolls removed across all iterations

input
text grid one row per line
all rows same length
only @ and . characters

output
two numbers part 1 result and part 2 result
